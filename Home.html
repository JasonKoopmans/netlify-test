Services

At Poynette Inch United Methodist Church, we have services for all. In addition to our traditional services held each Sunday, we have the following services and activities:

Wednesday Night

Wednesday Night Church School (WNCS) is for children aged 4K through 8th grade of members and non-members. For more information, please contact Michele Koopmans at 608.225.4305 or michele.koopmans@gmail.com

Small Group Study

Come and join our group study. Please contact Andy Ross at 608.444.6370 for more information including materials and meeting schedule.

Food Pantry

Our food pantry serves the villiage of Poynette and the surrounding areas. Items are always in need. For information on donations please contact Ruth Ann Waugh at 608.635.4167

Prayer Requests

Contact Poynette Inch at 608.635.2424 or poynetteinchumc1@gmail.com for prayer needs or to arrange a visit.

Wedding and Funeral

Church facilities are available for services upon request Please contact Pastor Dianne or one of our trustees for details and scheduling.
